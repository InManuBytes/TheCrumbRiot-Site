Meet the Chef

The Crumb Riot is where fine dining precision meets a playful kind of chaos. I'm a Colombian pastry chef trained in fine dining restaurants and luxury hotels with over 15 years of experience.

From plated desserts to decadent cookies, my work blends experimental flavor combinations with Latin American influences that delight with surprise.
